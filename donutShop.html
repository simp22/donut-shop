Simply Donuts

Taking Care of the Emerald City Since 1969

This is a simulator of a donut shop's production for a single day. The objective is to develop the program with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

There are two types of data:

  1. Summarized Data
  2. Detailed Data

The program focuses on a donut shop object with properties and methods and then building instances from the constructor function. These instances in this particular case are the individual locations or franchises.
